## Academic Papers and Research

### Perception Systems
- Fox, D., Burgard, W., & Thrun, S. (1997). "The Dynamic Window Approach to Collision Avoidance" - Foundational paper on local navigation with dynamic obstacles
- Scaramuzza, D., & Fraundorfer, F. (2011). "Visual Odometry: Part I: The First 30 Years and Fundamentals" - Comprehensive overview of visual odometry techniques
- Behley, J., et al. (2019). "SemanticKITTI: A Dataset for Semantic Scene Understanding of LiDAR Sequences" - Dataset for semantic segmentation with LIDAR data

### Planning Algorithms
- LaValle, S. M. (2006). "Planning Algorithms" - Comprehensive book on motion planning algorithms
- Karaman, S., & Frazzoli, E. (2011). "Sampling-based algorithms for optimal motion planning" - RRT* and other sampling-based planning methods
- Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (2016). "Springer Handbook of Robotics" - Comprehensive handbook with chapters on motion planning

### Control Systems for Humanoid Robots
- Kajita, S., et al. (2003). "Biped walking pattern generation by using preview control of zero-moment point" - Zero-moment point control for stable walking
- Takenaka, T., et al. (2009). "Real time motion generation and control for biped robot" - Real-time walking pattern generation
- Hofmann, A., et al. (2006). "Balance control for dynamically walking machines" - Dynamic balance control for walking robots

## Books

### Robotics and Navigation
- "Probabilistic Robotics" by Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, and Dieter Fox - Comprehensive coverage of probabilistic approaches to robotics
- "Springer Handbook of Robotics" by Bruno Siciliano and Oussama Khatib - Comprehensive handbook with chapters on navigation and locomotion
- "Introduction to Autonomous Mobile Robots" by Roland Siegwart, Illah Nourbakhsh, and Davide Scaramuzza - Introduction to mobile robotics concepts

### Humanoid Robotics
- "Humanoid Robotics: A Reference" by Ambarish Goswami and Prahlad Vadakkepat - Comprehensive reference on humanoid robotics
- "Human Walking" by Synergetics of Human Movement by Reinhard Preuss - Understanding human locomotion principles
- "Biomechanics and Motor Control of Human Movement" by David A. Winter - Understanding human movement for robotics applications
